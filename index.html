Excessive Use of Fertilizers

Fertilizers containing nitrogen and phosphorus are often applied in amounts far beyond what plants need. Excess nutrients enter water systems through runoff, triggering algal blooms and eutrophication — a growing threat to ecosystems and sustainable food production.
